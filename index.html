About Me


Dr. Ankita received her Ph.D. degree in Chemistry from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, UK, India, in 2018.

Her doctoral research focused on the design, development, and structure-property relationship of Naphthalimide-based organic materials for applications in optoelectronics and bioimaging.

Dr. Saini has actively contributed to the scientific community by publishing research papers, books and book chapters. She actively presented at and attended national and international conferences, workshops, seminars, MOOCs, NPTEL Courses and FDPs.

She has been recognized as an expert in her field, having been invited to serve as a speaker and session chair for lectures.

With a dedicated career in academia, Dr. Saini has served as an assistant professor of chemistry for six years. In this role, she continues to make significant contributions to education and research.
